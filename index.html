SolrDose Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026

SolrDose is a medication reminder app. This policy explains what data the app handles — which is simple, because everything stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. SolrDose has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, and no third-party services. We never see your data.

Data stored on your device

The medications, schedules, dose history, and settings you enter are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. They are never transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app permanently deletes this data.

Notifications

SolrDose uses Apple's local notification system to remind you about doses. Notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device.

Health information

Medication information you enter is used solely to show your schedule and reminders inside the app. It is not shared with the developer, Apple, or anyone else.

Children

SolrDose does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
