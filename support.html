SolrDose Support

A simple pill tracker with gentle reminders.

Contact

For help, bug reports, or feature requests, email solylu@proton.me. You'll usually hear back within a few days.

Common questions

I'm not getting reminders.

Check Settings → Notifications → SolrDose and make sure notifications are allowed. Also check that Quiet mode isn't on inside the app (Reminders tab), and that Focus/Do Not Disturb isn't silencing them.

How do I edit or delete a medication?

Open the Meds tab, tap the medication, then use Edit or Delete in the top-right corner.

Where is my data stored?

Only on your device. SolrDose has no accounts and sends nothing anywhere. Deleting the app deletes your data.

Can I change how often I'm nudged about a missed dose?

Yes — Reminders tab → "Nudge me again every" to pick 15, 30, or 60 minutes.
